What Is a Guarantee Stateroom?

A guarantee stateroom means you're locked into the cabin category — interior, ocean view, or balcony — but MSC assigns the specific room number closer to sailing. You won't know exactly where on the ship you'll be until then.

If the exact location doesn't matter to you, this is the more affordable way to book the same room type and amenities.
