Option 1
The Westin Reserva Conchal
An All-Inclusive Golf Resort & Spa · Playa Conchal, Guanacaste
All-Inclusive
Adults Only Club

If you want the most complete all-inclusive beach experience Costa Rica has to offer, this is the one. The Westin sits on Playa Conchal — named for its crushed-shell sand, which gives the water a clearer, blue-green color you don't find on most Costa Rica beaches. It's a bigger resort, with multiple pools and dining options, golf, and a full program of non-motorized water sports. The Westin Club category bumps you into a quieter adults-only section with its own lounge and pool, dedicated concierge, and private check-in. Liberia airport is about an hour away. For a ladies' trip where ease and beach quality are the priority, this one delivers.

Option 2
Secrets Papagayo Costa Rica
Adults Only · All-Inclusive · Playa Arenilla, Gulf of Papagayo
All-Inclusive
Adults Only

Secrets Papagayo is a smaller, more intimate all-inclusive built into a hillside overlooking the Gulf of Papagayo. The scale here is different — fewer rooms, a boutique feel, great ocean views from almost everywhere on the property. The beach at Playa Arenilla sits in a protected cove: the water is calm, good for paddleboarding and kayaking, and the sunsets from that bay are genuinely worth the trip. What it trades for beach quality (it's not Conchal-level sand) it earns back in atmosphere and proximity to the airport — about 20–30 minutes from Liberia. Dining rotates through seafood, Italian, Asian, and a buffet, no reservations required. One of the stronger romance-and-relaxation all-inclusive options in Guanacaste. Note: the pricing below reflects a 2-adult rate; final pricing for 3 adults will be confirmed.

Option 3
El Mangroove Papagayo, Autograph Collection
Marriott Autograph Collection · Gulf of Papagayo, Guanacaste
Room Only
Boutique Luxury

El Mangroove is the independent spirit of this group — a boutique Marriott Autograph Collection property with a small number of suites, modern Costa Rican design, and some of the best food in the region. It sits on Playa Panama, one of the calmer swimmable beaches in Papagayo, with a large oceanfront pool and genuinely good daytime vibes. Because this is room-only (not all-inclusive), meals come à la carte — which means you're free to eat at the resort or explore local spots in Guanacaste. The airport is about 20 minutes away. This is the right pick if your group wants a luxury base that doesn't feel like a resort complex, with excellent food and fewer crowds. Budget separately for meals and activities.

Option 4
Andaz Costa Rica Resort at Peninsula Papagayo
By Hyatt · Peninsula Papagayo, Guanacaste
Room Only
Top Tier Luxury

The Andaz is in a different category. Set within the private Peninsula Papagayo community — a gated development with multiple resort brands, golf, and private beaches — it's the most luxurious option in this quote and arguably the most impressive property in Costa Rica. The room feels closer to a high-end Hawaiian retreat than a traditional resort: monkeys wander the grounds, the suite has real bay views, and the dining (Rio Bhongo, Ostra, Chao Pescao) is some of the strongest in the region. Three pools, including an adults-only infinity pool. About 35–40 minutes from Liberia. The $533 resort fee is paid directly at check-in. This is the right choice if your group wants the absolute best experience in Costa Rica — rooms, food, setting, service — and meals à la carte are comfortable for your budget.
