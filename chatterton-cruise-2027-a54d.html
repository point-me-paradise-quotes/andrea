Where You're Going

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Nine nights on Royal Caribbean's Icon of the Seas — the largest cruise ship afloat — departing Miami and working south through the southern Caribbean before returning via Galveston. Two proper port days bookend the sea days: CocoCay on day two (Royal Caribbean's private island with its own waterpark and beach club), then Curaçao's Willemstad — a UNESCO World Heritage town where the Dutch colonial architecture has been painted every color imaginable — and Aruba on back-to-back days for beaches, windsurfing, and Zeerovers fish shack if you want the most local meal on the island.
