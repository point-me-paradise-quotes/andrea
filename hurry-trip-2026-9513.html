Choosing Between These Four

Barceló Maya Palace is the value play with the biggest connected-resort footprint. Hard Rock leans into water park thrills for kids who want to be wet all day. Iberostar splits the difference with a lazy river and heavy activity programming. Dreams Tulum is the only one with a Preferred Club upgrade included, trading some of the sprawl of the others for a more personal, service-forward stay.
