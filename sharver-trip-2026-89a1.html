The honest difference

What actually separates these two hotels

The Edgewater
  • 🌊 Built out over the water — not near it
  • 🚢 Pier 66 cruise terminal is next door
  • 🐟 Six-Seven restaurant: Pacific NW seafood on the water
  • 🛍 8 min walk to Pike Place Market
  • 🏔 Olympic Mountain views on clear days
  • ⬆ Higher price point — premium is the location
The Maxwell
  • 🎡 Space Needle half a block away
  • 🎨 Chihuly Glass Garden across the street
  • 🎸 MoPOP (kids love it) — 3 min walk
  • 🍕 Seattle Center restaurants within steps
  • 🏊 Pool on property — great with kids
  • 😄 Boutique, playful — staff, rooms, vibe
  • 💰 Significantly lower rate — more budget for activities

Both require a short ride to the cruise terminal (~10 min). The Maxwell has a pool — a real plus with four kids in tow. The Edgewater wins on atmosphere and proximity to the pier; the Maxwell wins on sightseeing access, the pool, and value for a family that wants to be out exploring.
