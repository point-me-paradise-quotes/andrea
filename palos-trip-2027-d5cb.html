Garden Spa Suite

King bed, a furnished terrace facing the gardens, a hydro spa tub, and 24-hour room service. It's built for the days you're not out at a park — a real place to land, not just a place to sleep.

Why this one

This isn't a beach-chair-and-book resort. Your stay includes unrestricted access to Xcaret, Xel-Há, Xplor, Xplor Fuego, Xenses, Xoximilco, Xenotes, and Xavage — Grupo Xcaret's own parks, plus transportation to all of them. If Yesenia wants a trip with a full daily itinerary of ziplining, cenotes, and cultural shows rather than five days of stillness, this is the property that's built around that.

  • Unlimited dining across multiple chef-driven restaurants
  • Unlimited premium beverages
  • Round-trip airport transportation included
  • Full access + transport to all Xcaret parks and select cultural tours
  • Kayaking, paddleboarding, and other non-motorized water sports
  • Daily art, culinary, dance, and wellness workshops
Transfers: included in the package price.

King bed, Mayan-inspired décor, a furnished balcony or terrace, and a double-vanity bathroom with a rainfall shower. Minibar refreshed daily, room service around the clock.

Secrets Tulum sits back in the jungle, then shuttles you to its own private stretch of Tulum's beach — so the resort itself is quiet and green, and the beach is a short ride away rather than out the front door. It's the middle option here on price, and it reads as the most "resort" of the three: pools, a full drink and dining program, nightly entertainment.

  • Unlimited dining, no reservations required
  • Unlimited premium spirits, wine, beer, soft drinks
  • 24-hour room service

  • Daily activities, nightly live entertainment
