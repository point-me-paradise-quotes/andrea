Day 1
Friday, October 9, 2026 — Arrival Day
Hotel Canopy by Hilton Portland Pearl District

The Pearl District is Portland's converted-warehouse arts neighborhood — design studios, independent coffee roasters, and around sixty breweries within walking distance. The Canopy sits in the middle of it. The building is newer, the rooms are clean and quiet, and the location puts you within ten minutes of Powell's City of Books on foot, the weekend Saturday Market along the waterfront, and easy access to streetcar lines heading downtown or toward the east side.

The hotel is also about 45 minutes from St. Helens — the town that becomes Halloweentown each October, with street vendors, costumed characters, and the filming locations from the 1998 movie. A midsize SUV and a Saturday morning drive is all it takes.
