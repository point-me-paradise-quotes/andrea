Andrea's Take

All three sailings hit Santorini with a long day there, so that part's covered no matter what you pick. The real decision is what you're optimizing for.

If the girls having things to do onboard matters most, Odyssey wins — it's the only one with the North Star, RipCord and bumper cars, and it's the shortest trip at 7 nights for the lowest deposit.

If you want the most ground covered — Santorini, Athens, Rhodes, Mykonos, plus Sicily and Croatia — Mariner is the pick, but it's 10 nights, open-jaw flights, and the highest total by a wide margin.

If it's really about the islands themselves with the least time at sea, Celebrity Infinity gets you Mykonos, Rhodes, Santorini and Hydra in 7 nights with only one sea day, round-trip out of Athens — simpler flights, and it's the lowest total of the three, just without the onboard credit.
