The ship

One of the largest ships in the world, built around seven distinct neighborhoods.

Symphony of the Seas is an Oasis Class ship designed to blend adventure, relaxation, and entertainment in equal measure. Its seven neighborhoods cover everything from quiet garden walkways to a boardwalk lined with dining and shops.

For thrill-seekers, there's the Ultimate Abyss — the tallest dry slide at sea — plus The Perfect Storm waterslides, a FlowRider surf simulator, a zip line, rock climbing walls, laser tag, and an ice rink. In the evenings, Broadway-caliber productions, AquaTheater shows, live music, and comedy round things out.

For downtime, there are multiple pools, the adults-only Solarium, and the Vitality Spa. Dining runs from complimentary main dining to a long list of specialty venues, so there's something for nearly every craving and budget.
