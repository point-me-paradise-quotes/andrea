A Quick Note on Guaranteed Rooms

The rates above are for guaranteed staterooms. That means Royal Caribbean assigns your exact cabin — deck, location, and number — sometime before sailing. You're locked into the room category you book, but not the specific spot on the ship.

Most families pick guaranteed cabins because they're consistently the lowest price within each category. If having a specific deck, being near an elevator, or booking connecting rooms matters more to you than the savings, we can price any of these three categories with an assigned cabin instead — just at a higher rate.
